About Suryapet

Suryapet district is carved out of erstwhile Nalgonda district. The district shares boundaries with Nalgonda, Yadadri, Khammam, Hanumakonda, Mahabubabad districts and Andhra Pradesh. The district comprises 23 mandals, 3 revenue divisions (Suryapet, Kodad, Huzur Nagar), 486 villages, and 5 municipalities. Suryapet town is a major road junction on National Highway 65, located exactly between Hyderabad (Telangana capital) and Vijayawada (Andhra Pradesh capital). The district has a population of 1,099,560 and a literacy rate of 64.11%.

History & Heritage

Ancient Roots

Suryapet has a rich historical background with evidence of human settlement dating back centuries. The region was part of various dynasties that ruled over Telangana.

Geographic Significance

Located strategically in the heart of Telangana, Suryapet serves as a major transportation hub connecting various parts of the state and neighboring regions.

Educational Growth

Over the years, Suryapet has emerged as a prominent educational center with numerous colleges and institutions contributing to the region's development.
